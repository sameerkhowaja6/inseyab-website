Our Story, Vision and Values

Achieve transformation with our AI-first, no-code approach — turning data into impact.

Inseyab was founded to remove barriers between organizations and the intelligence hidden in their data. While data drives innovation and progress, many enterprises and government entities still struggle to extract timely, actionable insights due to complex, slow, and expertise-heavy BI tools.

Founded by experienced AI, data science, and BI professionals, Inseyab was built on a clear vision: enable intuitive, AI-augmented decision-making without technical complexity. Our AI-powered no-code and low-code BI solutions transform raw data into actionable insights by embedding advanced analytics, machine learning, and automation—empowering decision-makers to understand, predict, and act with confidence.
